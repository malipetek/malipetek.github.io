PDF Extractor Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 3, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

PDF Extractor is designed with privacy as our top priority. The app handles documents locally and does not collect personal data.

No Data Collection

  • No personal information collection.
  • No usage analytics.
  • No device-level tracking.
  • No file content leaves your device.

Local Processing Only

  • Works completely offline.
  • No network required for core processing.
  • No cloud services for extraction.

File Handling

  • No uploads.
  • No file analysis is transmitted.
  • No retention after processing.

No Third-Party Services

  • No analytics SDKs.
  • No advertising SDKs.
  • No crash reporting.

Security

PDF Extractor runs locally and is signed/certified as needed for secure distribution.
